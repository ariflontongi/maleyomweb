Ice Spice Posts Nearly-NSFW Snapchat Video And Puts Some Fans In A State Of Disbelief

August 04, 2024
post

Ice Spice has had an incredible last 12 months. However, her run started before that thanks to her Fall 2022 hit "Munch." Overall, this song pushed her into superstardom, and she has been ascending ever since. She has put out songs with Taylor Swift, Nicki Minaj, and even Pinkpantheress. Moreover, she has her debut album coming out this year, Y2K. Although the album doesn't have a release date, it is still being heavily anticipated. At this point, fans believe it could be out as soon as the Summer.

Yesterday, Ice Spice took to Twitter where she told her fans to go follow her on Snapchat. While Snapchat may not be as popular as it once was, it is still used by Gen Z. Consequently, Spice saw an influx of followers yesterday and they were paying close attention to her Snap story. One of her videos eventually went extremely viral on Twitter, as blue checks attempted to aggregate some content. In this clip, Ice Spice is making some food, all while wearing a crop top with no bra on.

Ice Spice On Snapchat

The shirt is a bit see-through, and fans could tell that she has some piercings in some sensitive areas. Ice Spice is already someone that fans thirst over, and that was put into overdrive with this video. Below, you can see there were a ton of comments on the video, with fans going to great lengths to profess their attraction towards her. Overall, it yet another example of how the internet has very little chill over these kinds of things.
