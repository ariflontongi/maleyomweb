Princess Beatrice Looks So Lovely in a White Floral Dress in Saint-Tropez

August 29, 2024
post

Princess Beatrice took her regal style to Saint-Tropez for a dreamy getaway with husband Edoardo Mapelli Mozzi.

The pair were spotted walking around the harbor in the French Riviera town yesterday. Beatrice looked lovely in a breezy white sundress with a pink-and-green floral print. The piece was cinched at the waist and featured long puff sleeves and an ample skirt. She seemed to accessorize only with a brown clutch bag. (See the photos here.)

Edoardo coordinated with his wife in an airy white collared shirt, which he rolled up at the sleeves, brown trousers, and brown sunglasses.

MEGA

The princess and the property developer were pictured sharing a sweet embrace as they waited for a boat.

The pair attended the wedding of Edoardo’s stepsister last weekend in Cumbria, in northwest England. Daughter Sienna served as a flower girl while son Wolfie was a page boy, per reports.

The couple, who married in July 2020 at Windsor, honeymooned in France. A source told People at the time that they had to scale down their ceremony and honeymoon due to the COVID-19 pandemic, but had “a longer more luxurious honeymoon planned for August.”

Beatrice’s latest summer day-date look comes after she debuted two fabulous floral dresses at the Royal Ascot last month. One was a vintage-inspired white midi dress from Beulah London covered in a delicate pink-and-green rose floral print. The piece featured a ruffled mock turtleneck and puff bishop sleeves. To accessorize, the royal carried a white bucket bag with a circular tortoiseshell handle and wore a hot-pink bow headband from Juliette Botterill. She finished the elevated look with black-and-beige Chanel slingback pumps.

The other was an elegant long-sleeved white lace midi dress by Monique Lhuillier with an intricate lace overlay with violet, green, and yellow floral spots interspersed throughout. She coordinated with a cream fascinator with floral and fishnet adornments, plus a white handbag from Anya Hindmarch, pale pink satin pumps from Gianvito Rossi, and round diamond stud earrings.
