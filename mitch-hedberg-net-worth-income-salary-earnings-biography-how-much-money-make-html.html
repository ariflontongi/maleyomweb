Mike Hedberg (February 24, 1968 – March 29, 2005) was an American stand-up comedian noted for his bizarre comedy and unique comic style. Hedberg was born in Chicago, Illinois, and grew up in New York City. His humour was characterised by brief, often one-line gags that were interspersed with strange elements and non sequiturs, among other things. A cult following developed around Hedberg’s humour and stage presence, with audience members occasionally yelling out the punchlines to his jokes before he could complete them.
