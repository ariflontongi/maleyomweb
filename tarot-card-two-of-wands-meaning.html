Two of Wands’ Meaning In A Tarot Reading

Having moved beyond the spark of inspiration, the architect sets to work. Pen and paper, compass, and ruler are applied to sculpt out the shape of his dreams.

He makes discoveries along the way, makes critical decisions, and watches as he progresses along the path to creation. This is what the Two of Wands of the Minor Arcana implies. Know what this Rider Waite tarot card means in both the upright and inverted positions.

Two of Wands Tarot Card Meanings – Reversed

Overview:

The plan envisioned in the Ace of Wands is maturing. You’re expanding your boundaries and are being driven forward by the need to bring your vision into reality. The decisions are being made to stick with known paths or take a chance and go large. Just remember, big returns require big investments!

Past Events:

You’ve spent the past working on sketching out the details of a plan; you’ve laid the foundations and have done good work. All the decisions have been made, and it’s time to put them into action!

Present Events:

Beginnings are times for caution, but they are also powerful times. You create the cradle for your brainchild, the place where it will grow and bring your dreams to fruition. Knowing that this is a critical time, take care!

Future Events:

The time is not yet ripe for you to begin planning for that dream of yours. But that time is coming; for now, let it rest and wait for the explosion of inspiration that will keep you up nights, sketching, planning, and plotting.

Tarot Yes – No Interpretation Of Upright Two of Wands

This is an excellent position to start from; if this Minor Arcana tarot card has appeared, it’s letting you know that the answer is Yes, but you’re at the beginning of that Yes. Everything else is up to you from here on out.

Two of Wands Tarot Card Meanings – Upright

Overview:

The two wands are reversed to indicate a need for organization and a need to start over. Often partway through a plan, we’ll find that we’ve been wandering all over the place, and we need to bring ourselves back into focus to make sense of our creative ramblings. Pull back and start over is the lesson being imparted here.

Past Events:

Too much stuff! The past has seen you rambling and writing and scribbling, but there’s been no solid direction. This is an indicator that your past efforts may have been productive but not effective. It’s time to focus.

Present Events:

Chaos reigns. Your current creative energies are all over the place, and like a poorly insulated heating vent, you’re losing as much as you’re getting effective use out of. Take a moment to step back, take a breath, and pick one project to work with.

Future Events:

Your momentum is building, and there’s a warning on the wind for you. If you don’t bind down and focus your energies now, you’ll pull in a million different directions by the time you hit full speed.  Set your course, pick a project, and keep yourself on task.

Tarot Yes-No Interpretation Of Inverted Two of Wands

The answer is no. Things are in a time of chaos and unsettlement, so whatever you are asking is equally chaotic. It may be able to flip into a state of yes, simply due to the inherently unstable nature of its current circumstances.

Summary

Your willingness to go the extra step to achieve success will be important once you think of a bright future. Two wands encourage you to push your ambitions as you gain momentum towards getting on the right path.
