‘Glee’ Season 4 Cast Portraits

  • Cast

    Who's missing from Glee's fourth season poster? Dianna Agron's Quinn and Jayma Mays' Emma, but both are poised to return.

  • Mercedes

     Amber Riley's Mercedes graduated from McKinely and was headed for L.A. to be a backup singer. 

  • Kitty

    Becca Tobin stars as new Cheerio Kitty. Will she be a rival to Brittany?

  • Sam

    Chord Overstreet returns to McKinley this season as a regular, playing Sam Evans. 

  • Kurt

    Chris Colfer's Kurt starts the season at McKinley High, helping Mr. Schue repopulate New Directions. But it won't be long before he joins Rachel in New York. 

  • Finn

    Cory Monteith's Finn was last seen headed for boot camp in Georgia, after enlisting in the Army in the third season finale. 

  • Blaine

    Darren Criss' Blaine will have his hands full joining every club under the sun as he struggles to fill time following Kurt's departure for the East Coast. 

  • Mike

    Harry Shum Jr.'s Mike was headed for the Joffrey ballet after gaining his father's blessing to pursue a career in the arts after graduation. 

  • Brittany

    Heather Morris' Brittany is doomed to repeat her senior year after the dingy Cheerio's 0.0 GPA failed to make the grade to graduate alongside girlfriend Santana (Naya Rivera).

  • Jake

    Jacob Artist joins the Fox hit as Jake Puckerman, Puck's younger half-brother. 

  • Sue

    Jane Lynch will take on new duties as Sue Sylvester this season, with the year starting off with her snarky cheerleading coach having given birth. 

  • Tina

    Jenna Ushkowitz is sans boyfriend Mike when Season 4 picks up, with Tina now one of the few original New Directions members. 

  • Cassandra

    Kate Hudson guest stars in a handful of early Season 4 episodes as Rachel's challenging NYADA dance instructor Cassandra July.

  • Artie

    Kevin McHale's Artie will take the reins and help pick who should be the glee club's new star in when the fourth season opens. 

  • Rachel

    Lea Michele's Rachel Berry finds herself in unfamiliar territory as she's navigating the world of New York and a new school where she's no longer the queen bee.

  • Puck

    Mark Salling's Puck barely graduated last season and was rumored to be heading West to open a pool cleaning business in L.A.

  • Will

    Matthew Morrison's Will is going to be tasked with repopulating New Directions as the club is tasked with starting over after winning it all last season. 

  • Marley

    Melissa Benoist joins Glee as Marley, the newest member of New Directions.

  • Santana

    After graduating, Naya Rivera's Santana headed for a cheerleading scholarship at the University of Louisville. Don't expect her to stay there for long. 

  • Brody

    Dean Geyer joins the series as Brody, an upperclassman at  NYADA whom Rachel turns to for help/
