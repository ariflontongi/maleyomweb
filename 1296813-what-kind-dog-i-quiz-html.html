Dogs are undoubtedly among the animals with which humans have the most interaction with. There are numerous types of dogs, depending on traits such as size, primary purpose, breed, and color. Once in a while, dog lovers will sit back and ponder over the question, “What kind of dog am I?” Take this 'what kind of dog are you' quiz to find out which breed suits you best.

Why you should take a what dog am I quiz

Before taking a 'what dog are you quiz', keep in mind that different types of canines are known for varied traits. These include loyalty, courage, tenacity, friendliness, playfulness, energy, and obedience, amongst many more. It is only curious for a dog lover to wonder what type of dog they would be if given a chance. The 'what kind of dog am I quiz' above tries to match your strongest traits to the dog that exhibits similar characteristics.

Common types of dogs

Generally, every dog falls into one of seven classifications. These include sporting, non-sporting, terrier, hound, herding, working, and toy groups. Here is a look at the groups as well as the distinctive traits of the animals belonging to each.

What kind of dog am I if I am relentless and courageous?

The answer is a hound dog. Hounds are known for their superior sense of smell, fantastic stamina, and heightened vision. In the past, these were common in chasing and hunting expeditions. However, in the modern-day, these hounds have become more of companions, a role in which their superior senses still help a lot. Here are some common breeds in this group.

  • Basenji
  • Basset Hound
  • Beagle
  • Bloodhound
  • Irish wolfhound
  • Rhodesian ridgeback
  • Whippet

What dog breed are you if you are loyal, social, and obedient?

You would be a working dog that helps in the day to day operations of police departments, military departments, health, anti-terror units, and other jobs. These animals have instincts and talents, which are then perfected through training. Here are some of the popular breeds under this category.

What kind of dog are you if you have strong instincts and a love for the outdoors?

The answer is a sporting dog. These animals are well known for their intelligence, alertness, energy, and instinct. They are often used for flushing out, retrieving, or locating game in water or on land. While most of these dogs are still able hunting partners, a vast majority have been trained to become the perfect companion animals.

You would be a toy group dog. These are often short dogs with huge personalities. They are renowned for their adaptability, affection, playfulness, and friendliness.
