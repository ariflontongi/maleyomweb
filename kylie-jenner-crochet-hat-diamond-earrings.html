Kylie Jenner Pairs Her Floppy Crochet Hat With a Sparkling Pair of Diamond Earrings

August 28, 2024
post

Kylie Jenner has delivered a style lesson on how to effortlessly mix and match accessories.

The cosmetics mogul, who joined older sister Kendall Jenner and close friend Hailey Bieber on a New Year’s trip to Barbados, recently posted photos from the tropical getaway on her Instagram account. Leading the carousel of pictures was a sun-kissed selfie of Kylie smiling wide on a yacht, in which she put forth her own take on high-low accessorizing.

This included one of last summer’s biggest trends: a floppy crochet sun hat. Kylie’s sunflower-yellow hat, from New York City–based label Memorial Day, features thin forest-green and sky-blue stripes. For a luxe touch, she finished off her accessories with a glittering pair of diamond drop earrings.

Instagram

The mom of two continued to show off her tropical style with another photo in the same post. Posing against an oceanside sunset, she wore a strapless white dress made of delicate lace.

Instagram

Kendall also shared photos of her and Bieber’s vacation wardrobes.

The 818 founder wore a glamorous interpretation of the classic naked dress: a see-through cream mesh gown from supermodel Elsa Hosk’s fashion brand, Helsa, featuring ruffle trim, a diamond cutout on the abdomen, ruched detailing at the center of the waistline, a high-low skirt, and a subtle train. To accessorize, she added a pair of Bottega Veneta drop earrings.

Meanwhile, Bieber wore a clingy white maxi dress with a low back silhouette, paired with her go-to pair of textured gold pendant earrings.
