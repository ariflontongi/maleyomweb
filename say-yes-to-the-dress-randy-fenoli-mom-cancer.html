Say Yes to the Dress' Randy Fenoli posts heartwarming video of his mom, 96, leaving last cancer radi

August 04, 2024
post

SAY Yes to the Dress star Randy Fenoli posted a heartwarming video of his mom, 96, leaving her last cancer radiation appointment on Instagram.

The emotional clip, which he posted yesterday, sees his mother Jeanette walking towards a ceremonial bell with the aid of a walker to celebrate the end of her treatment while hospital staff cheer and clap for her.

'Ringing the bell' is a gesture meant to signal joy for cancer patients that have come to the end of their radiation treatment or chemotherapy.

Taking to Instagram, Randy, 56, captioned the tear-jerking video: "Mom’'s final radiation treatment last week!"

The TLC star went on to praise her strength and courage throughout her treatment.

He continued: "At 96, she’s one heck of a strong woman! So blessed to have her in my life!"

The video left many of his 575,000 Instagram followers full of joy while they also commented in their droves to wish her well.

One penned: "One heck of a lady and a strong survivor!"

"God bless!!!! You get it from your mama," another wrote. "Amazing news!!! She’s super cool!"

A fourth shared: "That's so wonderful... god bless you randy and your mom.. stay safe xxx" (sic).

Randy announced in January one social media that his mother was diagnosed with breast cancer and at the time ask his followers to send their prayers as she underwent surgery.

"Me and my Mom who’s 96 and was recently diagnosed with malignant breast cancer," he penned on Twitter alongside a picture of the two of them in happier times.

"Her surgery is tomorrow morning. Please send prayers. Thank you."

MOST READ IN ENTERTAINMENT

Randy first shot to fame in 2007 when he first appeared on TLC's Say Yes to the Dress where he was the Fashion Director for Kleinfeld Bridal.

Although he remains a fixture on the series, Randy left his position at Kleinfeld in 2012 to front a number of TLC shows including Say Yes to the Dress: Randy Knows Best, Randy to the Rescue and Say Yes To The Dress America.

He also has a successful bridal fashion label, Randy Fenoli.
