Biography

Hila Klein (born December 12, 1986) is a prominent Israeli-American YouTuber, designer, and entrepreneur. She co-hosts the h3h3Productions YouTube and the H3 Podcast channel with her husband, Ethan Klein.

Hila Klein is also the founding CEO of Teddy Fresh, an American streetwear brand known for its vibrant and playful designs. Their YouTube channel boasts over 6 million subscribers and features commentary on internet culture, viral videos, and social issues.

Early Life & Education

Hila Klein, born on December 12, 1986, in Tel Aviv, Israel, and raised in Holon, is a YouTuber, designer, and influencer. She grew up with her brother, Moses Hacmon, a renowned photographer. Her parents, Donna Klein and Gary Klein.

Her heritage is a captivating blend of Maghrebi Jewish, Mizrahi Jewish, Ashkenazi Jewish, Italian, and Spanish roots, a rich tapestry that shaped her upbringing.

Hila Klein attended Shenkar College of Engineering and Design in Israel, majoring in graphic design, where she met her husband and creative partner, Ethan Klein. Together, they launched the h3h3Productions YouTube channel, known for its humorous and insightful content.

Despite their success, Hila remains a vocal educational advocate, encouraging her fans to pursue their passions and interests.

Career

The Kleins embarked on their YouTube journey in 2011 under h3h3Productions. Their content expanded to various channels, including the H3 Podcast, and they even became characters in a Payday 2 DLC to fund a copyright lawsuit defence.

In 2017, Hila Klein founded Teddy Fresh, a Los Angeles-based streetwear brand where she serves as CEO and principal designer. Teddy Fresh collaborated with cultural icons like SpongeBob SquarePants, Rick and Morty, Elton John, and Care Bears. In 2021, they hosted a pop-up event with Looney Tunes.

Hila Klein and her husband, Ethan Klein, gained fame on YouTube for their witty commentary on internet culture. They also ventured into podcasting and fashion. Despite controversies, they’ve thrived, and Hila is now a prominent figure in online entertainment. She’s also known for her artistic collaboration with Yurie Sekiya for Teddy Fresh.

Hila Klein‘s accolades include a Streamy Award in 2016 and a Shorty Award 2019. She continues to inspire as she explores new artistic frontiers.

Controversies

Hila Klein has encountered controversy during her career. In 2019, an old video resurfaced in which she used the N-word, leading to backlash. She apologised, acknowledging her past ignorance of the word’s offensiveness. Additionally, her partnership with Louis Vuitton drew criticism for promoting materialism and elitism.
