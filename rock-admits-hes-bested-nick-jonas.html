The Rock Admits Hes Been Bested By Nick Jonas

August 19, 2024
post

The Rock had lot of competition in the ring, but it turns out that none of those guys used to be a Disney star. Nick Jonas is a breed of foe that The Great One never had to face in the squared circle. If he would have, it looks like he might not have been successful.
The People’s Champ recently reacted to GQ Magazine’s decision for the most stylish man in 2018. Needless to say, The Rock was okay with taking the L if that’s what he needed to do.

“The GQ Magazine’s Most Stylish Man of 2018 comes down to big newlywed Nick Jonas and the big, brown, bald dude – who’s divorced. Cue my Sinatra and kindly pour me a double, f*ck it Im going dark.”

We’re willing to be that Nick Jonas wouldn’t have sold a Stone Cold Stunner half as well as The Rock ever did. So there are things that each guy is better at. Jonas just might be better at putting on clothes.
