If your face flakes in the winter months, you’ll know harsh, chilly air and drying indoor heat can wreak havoc on skin. In my experience, colder months are equally brutal, if not moreso, on hair. The elements sap my strands of moisture, leaving them dull, straggle-prone, and spiky at the ends. 

Vegamour, specifically its Hydr8 collection, has restored silkiness to my hair — and my faith in hydrating shampoos as a whole. I’m hardly alone in my love for the brand. Nicole Kidman, a Vegamour ambassador, calls its GRO Revitalizing Shampoo her “favorite.” Other honorable mentions: the best-selling, drug-free hair growth serum shoppers swear by, along with its nourishing Hydra8 Deep Moisture Hair Repair Mask and accompanying shampoo and conditioners.

Best of all, the aforementioned hair saviors are sold, conveniently, in bundles, which are up to 35 percent off. Hurry — the sale won’t last forever.

Best Vegamour Deals:

Vegamour Gro Hair Serum Three Pack

A true icon in the ever-widening hair-growth category, Vegamour’s serum is among the first I’d noticed garnering mass acclaim — and for good reason. Shoppers swear the lightweight, leave-in serum truly does what it claims. Vegamour Shampoo and Conditioner Set

Notably gentle, this in-shower cleansing set delivers a fresh feel without stripping strands of moisture. The indulgent cream softens my hair in minutes — even when it's haylike. Plus, it smooths straggling strands, leaving them softer and shinier after a single use.
