Starting his career in the 1980s in Paris nightclubs, he quickly made a name for himself with his unique blend of house music. His breakthrough came with the album "Just a Little More Love" in 2002, marking the beginning of a series of successful albums and singles that would redefine the genre.

His ability to collaborate with a range of artists, from emerging talents to established stars, has been a hallmark of his career. These projects contributed significantly to his financial success, helping to build his impressive net worth.
