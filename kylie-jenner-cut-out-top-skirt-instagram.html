Kylie Jenner Bares Her Midriff in a Cut-Out Top &amp; Mini Skirt

August 26, 2024
post

Following the release of new Keeping Up with the Kardashians footage which showed Kylie Jenner and Kendall Jenner having an altercation, Kylie took to Instagram to set the record straight.

Wearing a one-shoulder, cut-out dress featuring an exposed midriff section, Kylie captioned the photos, "i’m not bossy i’m the boss." It's entirely possible that Kylie is referencing the explosive fight she had with her sister Kendall, as the pair battled it out over who would wear a specific outfit on a night out in Palm Springs. Kylie won.

For her latest Instagram post, the Kylie Cosmetics founder wore Kim Shui's cut-out sleeve tie top in a tropical print, and a matching skirt, per @kyliejennercloset. As noted by the Instagram account, Kylie accessorized her revealing look with Aliel's Orange Ghalia purse, which retails for $320, Gianvito Rossi's gold metallic strappy heels, and Ruby Stella's gold Cuban link ring. She also wore the Daneuse Étoile ring and the Revolution necklace, both by Dior.
