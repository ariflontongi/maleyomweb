After the Royal Family touched down in Canada on Saturday evening, the Duke and Duchess attended the Official Welcome Ceremony for the Royal Tour at the British Columbia Legislature. Kate wore a blue Jenny Packham dress, adorned with the Queen's platinum and diamond maple leaf brooch, and a matching fascinator hat by Sylvia Fletcher that also featured subtle maple leaf details as an ode to her destination. She completed the elegant ensemble with a pair of nude suede Gianvito Rossi pumps and an L.K. Bennett clutch.
