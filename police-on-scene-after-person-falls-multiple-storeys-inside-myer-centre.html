Myer Centre Adelaide: Person falls multiple storeys inside shopping centre

August 15, 2024
post

A person has fallen multiple storeys inside the Myer Centre in Adelaide’s Rundle Mall.

Police and ambulance are on the scene.

The Myer Centre is closed and parts of Rundle Mall are blocked off by emergency services.

The MFS, police and ambulance services are all in attendance and a cherry picker has entered the centre to retrieve the person.

Employees who work in the Myer Centre as well and patrons have been evacuated and are waiting outside for the all clear.

All entrances to the centre are closed.

It is not known at this stage the circumstances that led to the fall but it is not believed to be suspicious.
