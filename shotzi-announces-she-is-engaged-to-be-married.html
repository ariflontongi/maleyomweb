Shotzi Announces She Is Engaged To Be Married

August 21, 2024
post

Shotzi Blackheart had a memorable time in NXT, and she managed to become a fan-favorite there. Her character work and in-ring skills helped her reach that point as well. She is going through a whirlwind of emotions this past week and now it appears Shotzi is officially engaged to be married.

Shotzi Blackheart shocked fans during SmackDown as she shaved her head. This came as surprise for fans for many reasons. Blackheart then revealed that she did it to pay tribute to her sister, who is battling cancer.

The Smackdown Superstar also recently shared more incredible news as she took to her Instagram story and uploaded a video of herself getting proposed by her longtime partner. Naturally, she said yes and is now engaged to be married. However, she seems to have deleted the video for some reason.

We would like to congratulate Shotzi Blackheart on the happy occasion and it is certain fans can’t wait to see her getting married as soon as possible. We’ll have to see what WWE will have planned for her in the meantime.
