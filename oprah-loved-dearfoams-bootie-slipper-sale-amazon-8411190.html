Oprah's Go-To Cozy Slippers Are on Amazon Sale Starting at $26

August 12, 2024
post

If there’s one thing we’ve learned about Oprah Winfrey over the years, it’s that she loves all things cozy. The media mogul consistently features a Cozy Gifts section full of comfy clothes, shoes, and home goods on her annual Favorite Things list — including the newly released 2023 version. And right now, the Dearfoams Warm Up Bootie Slippers that Winfrey highlighted in 2022 are on sale starting at $26 on Amazon. 

Available in six colors, the booties are made from quilted nylon with memory foam insoles that “feel like you’re walking on a cloud,” Winfrey wrote. Plus, they’re completely lined with plush, faux shearling to keep your feet and ankles warm, and they have textured rubber outsoles, so you can wear them both inside and out. The slippers come in sizes S through XL, which corresponds to shoe sizes 5 through 12, per the brand. 

In addition to their durable construction and toasty interior, the best part of these slippers is that they’re machine washable. A shopper even confirmed that “they last forever, even after countless washings.” That means you can confidently wear them both around the house and outside without worrying about ruining or dirtying the fabric. 

Tons of other shoppers gave the booties a five-star rating and wrote glowing reviews. One person, who was “influenced by Oprah” to buy the slippers, said they feel “like a warm, soft hug for your feet.” Another reviewer described the shoes as “little sleeping bags on your feet,” and someone else confirmed they’ll keep your feet “toasty warm without being sweaty and uncomfortable.” 

Many reviewers also commented on the slippers’ versatile design and quality construction. A shopper described them as “cozy and comfy for the upcoming chilly winter, but durable enough to run errands and walk the dog.” And, as a second reviewer put it, the booties are “so well made and adorable.” 

Before word gets out about this sale, be sure to grab a pair of the Oprah-loved Dearfoams Warm Up Bootie Slippers from Amazon, and check out more styles from the brand, below.
