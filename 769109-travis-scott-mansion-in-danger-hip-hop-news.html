Travis Scott's $23.5 Million Cali Mansion In Danger Of Damages Due To Severe Amounts Of Rain

August 18, 2024
post

Travis Scott has grinded hard over the course of his over decade-long career. The Houston, Texas rapper has gone from a Kanye West underling to a massively influential artist. He essentially created the psychedelic trap subgenre creating massive pieces of sonic art. From Rodeo, to ASTROWORLD, to 2023's UTOPIA, La Flame has perfected and experimented with his craftsmanship. All of this musical success has led him to be able to explore other lanes of business. Just recently, he unveiled Cacti Park. It is a baseball field in West Palm Beach that will play host to MLB Spring Training games. All of that money has most likely allowed Travis Scott to purchase an expensive California mansion.

Scott is always talking about living in the hills in his songs and lives out that fascination in reality. The 31-year-old superstar possesses a massive $23.5 million mansion in Brentwood. It sits stunningly on the side of hill with a gorgeous view of the neighborhood below. However, Scott's estate could be in some trouble.

Travis Scott Could Be In Trouble

According to XXL, there have record-breaking amounts of rainfall in the area with LA getting about eight inches of it in just three days at one point. Wind gusts have also hit about 102 mph according to the AP News. Additionally, over 475 mudslides were reported across the state as well. All of this accumulates into dangerous and weathered conditions for Scott's mansion on the hill. In the video above you can see the massive crack just feet away from it. Even more rain is expected this week and with the cracks already in about four feet of topsoil, an evacuation could be in order for Travis and the other residents on the same landscape.
