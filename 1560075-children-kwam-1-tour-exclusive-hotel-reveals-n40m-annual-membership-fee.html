Not for Children: KWAM 1 Gives Tour of His Exclusive Hotel, Reveals N40m Annual Membership Fee

Legendary Fuji musician King Wasiu Ayinde Marshall, aka KWAM 1, has left many netizens drooling over his new hotel.

The wealthy musician gave guests a tour of the beautiful property, and the video was posted online by actor Koko Zaria.

In the video, KWAM 1 showed off all the impressive facilities in his establishment and was also heard bragging about bringing specialists from overseas to provide great service to his guests.

Speaking further, the Fuji maestro noted that his hotel was not exactly open to the general public and runs on a membership basis. According to him, members would have their food and accommodation covered.

He went ahead to reveal that it cost at least N25 million to N40 million to be a member of his establishment for a year.

KWAM 1 said:

“What we run here is not per se to the general public, we do membership only, strictly membership… when you are a member, we have prices ranging from N25 million to N30- N40 million annually, free food and accommodation.”
