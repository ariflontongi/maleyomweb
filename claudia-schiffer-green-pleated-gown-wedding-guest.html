Supermodel Claudia Schiffer shimmers in wedding-ready gown with sweetheart bodice

August 18, 2024
post

Even when she's not the centre of attention, Claudia Schiffer is still the belle of the ball. The German supermodel attended the wedding of Grace Morton, the vice president of production and development for Taylor Sheridan’s Bosque Ranch Productions, and Matthew Budman, a producer and partner at DreamCrew Entertainment. The sensational bash was hosted in the Caribbean, putting Claudia's impeccable summer style to the test.

The 52-year-old dazzled in an emerald gown featuring a pleated hem, a tight wrap-effect bodice, thick straps, a metallic finish and a stunning sweetheart neckline. She posed for a mid-wedding photo with the bride, who glowed in a white haute couture Chanel wedding dress complete with a corseted bodice, an off-the-shoulder silhouette, a tiered skirt and a unique bobbled effect.

In a touching twist, Grace's exquisite dress paid tribute to Claudia as it was inspired by her favourite Karl Lagerfeld-designed bridal look from a 1992 Chanel show which was originally worn by Claudia.

Completing her elegant emerald aesthetic, Claudia wore her hair down loose and opted for a light makeup touch that perfectly emphasized her career-defining features. A rainbow-hued, glitter-clad handbag was hooked over her shoulder for an extra pinch of pizzazz.

She shared the post with fans online, captioning the snaps from the Jamaican bash: "Read Vogue's article at the link in stories for a taste of the endless fun @g_mort’s Jamaican wedding was. Grace you are the epitome of the @chanelofficial bride, beautiful and glamorous. Matthew and Grace, your love is transcendental."

It seems that Claudia is the go-to source of style inspiration for socialite weddings. The star was cited as Nicola Peltz Beckham's inspiration for her wedding to Brooklyn Peltz Beckham in April 2022.

Nicola's bridal hair was inspired by the 90s supermodel, as revealed by hairstylist Adir Abergel, a longtime friend of Nicola's.
