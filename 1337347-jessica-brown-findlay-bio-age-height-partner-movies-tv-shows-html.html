Jessica Brown Findlay's childhood and education

Since an early age, Jessica had her eyes set on becoming a ballerina. She even managed to persuade her parents into helping her pursue ballet as a profession.

The actress underwent ballet training at the Associates of the Royal Ballet as well as the National Youth Ballet. Afterwards, she performed for a single summer season alongside the Kirov Ballet. After this, Brown joined the Furze Platt Senior School in Maidenhead for her formal education.

Jessica's ballet dream was unfortunately cut short when she developed ankle complications during her third year of study at the Arts Educational School in Hertfordshire. The complications forced her to undergo three operations, the last of which was unsuccessful.

Not willing to give up on art, the actress enrolled at the Central Saint Martins College of Art and Design for a course in Fine Art.
