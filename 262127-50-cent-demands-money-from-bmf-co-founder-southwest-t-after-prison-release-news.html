50 Cent Demands Money For BMF Co-Founder Southwest T After Prison Release

August 28, 2024
post

50 Cent has been working on a television show about BMF, short for Black Mafia Family, for the last few years. A major development was made in the story of the notorious drug trafficking organization this week when it was revealed that Terry "Southwest T" Flenory, one of the co-founders of BMF and Big Meech's brother, was released from prison due to COVID-19 concerns. It only took a few hours but Fif is already requesting money for Flenory, giving all of his foes a deadline on social media.

Posting a photo of Southwest T behind bars on Instagram, 50 Cent kindly requested for the man to get paid before next week, encouraging his rivals to take their time in getting to the bank.

"@bmfboss_swt_263 is out, take your time getting to the Bank, No hurry just have what you owe by Monday," said Fiddy. "The fuck you mean you ain’t got the money. you better get it,before he start looking at this shit a little different," he added in a second post.

Southwest T was released from prison today. He was well-known for overseeing Black Mafia Family's operations in California. His brother, Demetrius “Big Meech” Flenory, is also fighting for his own release from prison.
