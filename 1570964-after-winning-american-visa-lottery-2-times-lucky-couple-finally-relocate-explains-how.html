After Winning American Visa Lottery 2 Times, Lucky Couple Finally Relocate to US, Explains How

August 12, 2024

  • A Zambian couple has celebrated migrating to the United States after emerging winners of the diversity American visa lottery (Diversity Visa Program)
  • In a TikTok video, the wife shared how they applied and won twice and showed how they relocated with their kids
  • On the possibility of a family winning the American lottery twice, a US-based education consultant, Ifeanyi Eze, shared his thoughts on it

A Zambian woman, Wabei Mukwasiyo, has announced her family's relocation to the United States after they won the American visa lottery twice.

Taking to TikTok, she shared a clip of her family in an aeroplane as they first stopped in Qatar before flying to Atlanta, Georgia.

Wabei's family won the lottery twice

Responding to netizens who asked how they did it, Wabei revealed that they first applied in 2021 and won in 2022.

Again, they applied in 2022 and won in 2023 and finally left in January of the same year.

"Applied in 2021 won in 2022, I applied in 2022 again and won in 2023."We applied for diversity visa lottery in 2021. Won in May 2022 and travelled in January 2023," she wrote.

About their flight ticket and accommodation, Wabei gave an insight:

"There are institutions that offer accommodation services to Dv winners. So once you win and you don’t have a relative here you can apply."

Can a particular family win the lottery more than once?

When asked about the possibility of a particular family winning the American lottery consecutively, Ifeanyi Eze, an education consultant based in Atlanta, US, told Legit.ng:

"It is possible. The lottery system does not consider family ties. It treats applicants as an individual."
